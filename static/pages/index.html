Our Mission

We step in where the second chance starts.

Companions of CPAS exists to give dogs at Caddo Parish Animal Services the opportunity they may not get otherwise. As a 100% volunteer-based nonprofit, every dollar is directed toward helping animals who need care, transport, visibility, and a safe path forward.

Open Your Home

Meet your familys next best friend.

Fostering gives a dog safety, stability, and the time they need to be seen. It is one of the fastest ways to create space, save lives, and help a dog become ready for forever.

From Crisis to Care

Some dogs arrive with stories they cannot tell.

Road rash, illness, neglect, and urgent medical needs can make a dog easy to overlook in an overcrowded shelter. Companions of CPAS helps turn those moments into treatment, recovery, and a future.
